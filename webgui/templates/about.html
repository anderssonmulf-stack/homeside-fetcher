What We Monitor

Temperatures

Indoor, outdoor, supply, return, and hot water — tracked continuously to give a complete picture of your heating system.

Energy Consumption

Hourly and daily energy data, automatically imported and analyzed to reveal usage patterns and anomalies.

System Status

System pressure, operating mode, and equipment status — so you know immediately when something changes.

Weather Conditions

Current observations and forecasts, used to anticipate heating demand and optimize proactively.

Key Capabilities

  • Energy separation — automatically splits total consumption into heating and hot water
  • Thermal efficiency analysis — learns how your building responds to outdoor conditions
  • Weather integration — forecasts drive proactive adjustments before temperature shifts
  • Multi-property dashboards — manage your entire portfolio from one place
  • AI-powered insights — ask questions about your data in plain language
  • Alerting — get notified when readings fall outside expected ranges
